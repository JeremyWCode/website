Jeremy Williamson Family, a little Family History

Patrick Williamson, my Father

My father was born in Edmonton, October 16th, 1962 is half Cree from Northern Alberta, and also half Irish. My father worked as a Master Mechanic/Millwright for 4 Decades. He is also a Steam Locomotive Engineer and now works on his Gold Claim mining for gold in the Cariboo Region of British Columbia.

My Father's Father, George W.R. Williamson and Mother, Lucienne Williamson

George W.R. Williamson

George was born in Northern Ireland. In 1951, he served as a corporal for the Territorial Army of Northern Ireland. He attended Trinity College and Queen's University in Belfast obtaining his mechanical engineering degree. He was an avid rugby player and fan.
